    View and edit your Status profile¶

    Currently, some profile sections are not editable on Status desktop. Use the Status mobile app to edit them.

    Your profile serves as a showcase of who you are to other Status users. You can make as many edits as you need, and the profile updates take effect instantly.

    Tip

    To see how your profile appears to others, check out View your profile as someone else.

    Edit your Status profile¶

    1. From the tab bar, tap Messages.
    2. Tap your profile picture.
    3. Tap Edit profile.
    4. Choose the area you want to edit and follow the corresponding procedure from this table：
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click Settings, and then click Profile.
    2. In the Profile content area, select Edit.
    3. Choose the area you want to edit and follow the corresponding procedure from this table：
    Area to edit Availability
    Profile picture Mobile and desktop (limited functionality)
    Name, bio or accent colour Mobile and desktop (limited functionality)
    Communities, accounts, collectibles or assets Mobile
    Web links Mobile and desktop

    Profile picture¶

    You can use a photo, an image or a collectible as your profile picture. If you use Status desktop, you can only choose an image from your computer.

    1. Tap Camera in your profile picture.
    2. In the action sheet, choose Take photo, Select from gallery or Select collectible.
    3. Authorize the Status app to access your camera or photo gallery.
    4. Set a profile picture and tap Choose.
    1. Click Edit in your profile picture.
    2. Choose a picture from your computer.
    3. Adjust your picture size and click Make this my profile picture.

    Name, bio or accent colour¶

    Use Status mobile if you want to edit the accent colour.

    1. In the Profile list, choose the item to edit.
    2. Edit the content and click Save.
    1. Update content in the Display name or Bio field.
    2. Click Save changes.

    Communities, accounts, collectibles or assets¶

    Such information is hidden by default. You can choose what to reveal to different groups of Status users.

    1. In the Showcase list, choose Communities, Accounts, Collectibles or Assets.
    2. Tap the item you want to edit.
    3. Choose Everyone, Contacts, ID verified contacts or No one.

    Caution

    Be careful with sharing sensitive information to protect yourself against scams and fraud.

    Web links¶

    If you want to tell people more about yourself, you can display social media links and other web links on your profile.

    1. In the On the web list, tap Links.
    2. Choose the action you want to complete and follow the steps from this table:
    Action Steps
    Add links 1. Tap Add and select the type of link to add.
    2. If you want to add a website, type the name and URL. If you want to add your social media, type your username.
    3. Tap Save.
    Reorder links In the Links list, hold and drag the item to the position you want.
    Edit links 1. Tap the link you want to edit.
    2. Update the content and tap Save.
    Delete links 1. Swipe left over the link to delete and tap Delete.
    2. Tap Delete again to confirm.
    1. Choose the action you want to complete.
    2. Follow the steps from this table:
    Action Steps
    Add links 1. Scroll down and click + Add more links.
    2. Select the type of link to add.
    3. If you want to add a website, type the URL. If you want to add your social media, type your username.
    4. Click Add, then click Save changes.
    Reorder links 1. Scroll down and find the link to reorder.
    2. Hold and drag the item to the position you want.
    Edit links 1. Scroll down and find the link to edit.
    2. Click Edit.
    3. Type the new content and click Update.
    4. Click Save changes.
    Delete links 1. Scroll down and find the link to delete.
    2. Click Edit.
    3. Click Delete, then click Save changes.
